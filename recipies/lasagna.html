How to Prepare
  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, cook 1 pound of ground beef over medium heat until browned. Drain any excess fat.
  4. Add 1 chopped onion and 2 minced garlic cloves to the skillet. Cook until the onion is tender.
  5. Stir in 24 ounces of marinara sauce and let it simmer for about 10 minutes.
  6. In a medium bowl, mix together 15 ounces of ricotta cheese, 1 beaten egg, and 1/4 cup of grated Parmesan cheese. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Spread a thin layer of the meat sauce at the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  8. Place a layer of lasagna noodles over the sauce.
  9. Spread a layer of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles.
  10. Sprinkle a layer of shredded mozzarella cheese over the ricotta mixture.
  11. Repeat the layers (meat sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella) until all ingredients are used, ending with a layer of mozzarella cheese on top.
  12. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.
  13. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  14. Let the lasagna stand for about 10 minutes before serving to allow it to set.
