Asparagus and Egg

Description

Asparagus and eggs make the best spring breakfast or a beautiful light lunch. Asparagus is cooked in a pan with a little oil, and topped with two eggs.

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Asparagus Stalks
  • Eggs
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Red Pepper Flakes (optional)
How to Prepare
  1. Trim the ends of 1 bunch of asparagus and blanch them in boiling water for 2-3 minutes, until tender-crisp. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil over medium heat. Add the blanched asparagus and saut√© for 2-3 minutes, until lightly browned. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Push the asparagus to one side of the skillet. Crack 2-4 eggs into the skillet and cook until the whites are set and the yolks are still runny, about 3-4 minutes.
  4. Season the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper. Optionally, sprinkle some grated Parmesan cheese over the eggs and asparagus.
  5. Serve the asparagus and eggs immediately, garnished with fresh herbs such as parsley or chives if desired.
